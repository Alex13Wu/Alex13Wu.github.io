Before sunrise

2017-10-10

国庆回家看到大学打印的《before sunrise》剧本，还有那首诗。
毫无疑问这是我最最喜欢的一部电影，爱到从不愿和别人分享，谈起。
《爱在》三部曲让所有话痨电影黯然失色，特别是第一部。第二部与第一部隔了一个月就看了，让我后悔不已，应该五年后再看的，所以第三部我至今不舍的看。
《before sunrise》讲了两个人的相遇，Jesse大概是我最喜欢的角色了，那样幽默，帅气，带着痞痞的气质，真实！我承认我模仿过他，the way he spoke，even the way he dated with girl , so charming , so handsome , so profound . As the girl , so elegant , so pretty , so brave, so special …
I’ve download the script , and read it more than one time … I’ve seen the movie more than once , for godshake , I love it so much ! I don’t even want anyone others knew about it, cause it meant a lot to me.
“You have no idea where I came from we have no idea where we are going”
I like the poem that homeless guy wrote for them , even liked the way he “beg” .
Two people , walked and talked , never got bored , I used the said that’s so call “Soulmate”, but now I realize , everything is coincidence, and they are so lucky they met each other , even just one night , even they might never met again , so what ?
I like it when the girl said “You couldn’t know why a night like this is so important to my life right now”
I like it when the man talking about eternal life.
Jesse说：“如果我们一直待在一起，你就会开始讨厌我的坏习惯。。。”一语成谶，多年以后，，，well，更后面的发展还没看，也舍不得看，等我四十岁再看吧！
在第一遍没注意到Jesse最后读的W.H.Auden 的诗。后来背了抖森读的诗，甚至用那句
“o let no time deceive you , you can not conquer time ”
撩妹，经女友提醒才发现和他读的就是同一首。乐死我了！
BTW，男主伊桑霍克是我超级喜欢的男演员，从某些角度看他的某样长得太像我爸了，所以我也觉得我像他。
